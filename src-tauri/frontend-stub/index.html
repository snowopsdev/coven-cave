Connect to CovenCave

Your Cave lives on your desktop — this phone is a remote for it. On your Mac, open CovenCave, click the phone icon, then scan the QR code or paste the invite link here.

First time here? Both devices need Tailscale

CovenCave talks to your desktop over your private Tailscale network — no cloud in between. Three steps, once:

  1. Install the free Tailscale app from the App Store on this phone.
  2. Sign in with the same Tailscale account your desktop uses.
  3. Turn Tailscale on, then come back here and connect.
Saved connection
Enter Tailscale URL manually Use the plain .ts.net URL only after this app has already connected once or if CovenCave is running without an invite gate.
Can’t reach your desktop from this phone

Most likely causes, in order — fix the first that applies:

  1. Tailscale is off on this phone. Open the Tailscale app and connect (install it first if it’s missing).
  2. Your desktop is asleep. Wake it up.
  3. CovenCave isn’t open on the desktop. Open it and make sure Mobile mode is on in Settings → Phone.
